              - ## Irish Tax Rates Quick Reference (2025)
             
              - | Tax | Rate | Threshold |
              - |-----|------|----------|
              - | Income Tax (standard) | 20% | Up to €42,000 |
              - | Income Tax (higher) | 40% | Above €42,000 |
              - | USC Band 1 | 0.5% | Up to €12,012 |
              - | USC Band 2 | 2% | €12,012 – €25,760 |
              - | USC Band 3 | 4% | €25,760 – €70,044 |
              - | USC Band 4 | 8% | Above €70,044 |
              - | PRSI (employee) | 4% | All earnings |
             
              - ## Common Calculations
             
              - - **Standard employee** — PAYE + USC + PRSI deducted at source
                - - **Self-employed** — Preliminary tax + income tax return
                  - - **Rental income** — Added to total income, taxed at marginal rate
                    - - **ETF investments** — 41% exit tax, deemed disposal every 8 years
